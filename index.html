Tokenomics

Tokenomics refers to the economic design and structure behind a cryptocurrency or token within a blockchain project. It combines "token" and "economics" and defines how the token is issued, distributed, and used within its ecosystem. Ultimately, tokenomics ensures sustainable value, utility, and demand for the token, forming the foundation of a project’s economic stability and long-term success.
