Ship agency and port operations

Shipping touches everything in the modern world. 90% of the commodities traded worldwide have at some point been transported by water. We are alongside to ease the way your vessels and cargoes complete or began their sea passage.We go beyond the standard ship agency services with an extensive range of complementary services. We are at your full disposal 24/7 in all Bulgarian ports and we have you covered.

You can rely on us for, but not limited to, the following agency roles:

  • Owner’s Agency
  • Charterer’s Agency
  • Protective Agency
  • Husbandry/Technical Services
  • Dry-Docking Agency
  • Any Marine-Related Services

Road and rail transport

Ultra Maritime offers both reliability and flexibility with our customized inland cargo transport solutions. Our professionals combine your cargoes with the right mode of transport via the right route, with the possibility of restructuring if conditions change, striving to provide the most convenient transport organization in terms of time and cost.

We manage the process from end to end, while providing a solution to meet your needs and exceeds your expectations.

Freight forwarding and logistics

As a freight forwarder, Ultra Maritime prepares all the necessary documentation and procedures of the authorities and also performs the necessary due diligence to ensure that all regulatory and security requirements are met. Our company is dedicated to understanding customer expectations and identifying and developing opportunities that enhance customer supply chain strategies.

Our operating philosophy is founded on maintaining the highest level of quality service in the most efficient manner possible. We are committed to continuous process improvement through leadership, innovation, and technology.

Multimodal transport solution

In Ultra Maritime we understand that no two customers are entirely alike as well as their needs and requirements of transport solutions. Therefore, we develop tailor made multimodal transport solutions, which are designed to meet the specific requirements of our customers.

We use the expertise of our team to make your logistics more sustainable. Our combined transport solutions offers advantages such as increased transportation capacities, reduced costs, faster transit times and a smaller carbon footprint. We guarantee seamless, flexible and secure operations, even when shipping large volumes.

Customs brokerage

Avoid delays of your shipments in the customs clearance process. Our in-house customs experts provide expertise across tariff and Customs laws, rules and regulations for the clearance of imported or exported goods, the preparation of import or export documents including computation and payment of duties, taxes and other charges.

In addition, our customs consultants can review your supply chain to help improve customs activities, optimise customs duties, identify potential risk areas and optimise internal controls and procedures for maximum internal compliance.
